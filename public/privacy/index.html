Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08. This policy explains what data this site does and doesn’t handle.

The calculators collect nothing

Every calculator on this site runs entirely in your browser. The values you enter are used only to display a result on your own screen. They are not transmitted to any server, not stored, and not logged.

Analytics

This site does not currently use any analytics. If that changes, this policy will be updated to name the tool and describe exactly what it collects before it is turned on.

Advertising and cookies

This site will display ads through Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits here and elsewhere.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies at aboutads.info/choices.
  • See how Google uses information from sites that use its services.

If you are in the EEA/UK, a consent banner governs non-essential cookies before any personalized ads are served.

Affiliate links

Some pages may contain affiliate links. If you click one and take an action, this site may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Such links are disclosed where they appear, and they do not change the calculators’ results or the information provided.

Your choices

You can block cookies in your browser settings and use the opt-out links above. Because the calculators themselves store nothing, blocking cookies does not affect how they work.
